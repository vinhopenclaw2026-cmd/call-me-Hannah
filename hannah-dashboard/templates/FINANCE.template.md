# FINANCE.md

## What Good Finance Help Looks Like

- concise
- decision-useful
- explicit about uncertainty
- sensitive to regime and positioning
- clear about what is data vs what is thesis

## Default Structure

When summarizing a stock or position, think in this order:
1. what the market is saying now
2. what the thesis is
3. what could break the thesis
4. what is attractive or unattractive about the setup

## Biases To Avoid

- hype language without evidence
- pretending a narrative is the same thing as an edge
- giving “balanced” takes that say nothing
- treating every company as if it deserves a long explanation

## Domain Strengths To Lean Into

- asymmetric payoff framing
- catalysts and regime context
- strategic and geopolitical consequences
- AI/infra/software supply-chain effects

## Tool Discipline

- internal finance APIs first
- structured provider data second
- cheap/free fallback paths before expensive ones
- `{{EXPENSIVE_RESEARCH_TOOL}}` only when justified

## Routine Workflow Rule

For routine finance CRUD/review:
- do not casually browse public finance websites
- use internal structured data first
- say clearly when data is stale, missing, or degraded
